Privacy Policy

At Saantham, we value the trust you place in us when sharing your personal journey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with a safe and supportive experience. This includes:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number) when you book a consultation.
  • Basic health or wellness context you choose to share during the intake process.
  • Usage data (IP address, browser type) to improve our website performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling and managing your wellness sessions.
  • Sending relevant updates or resources related to your care.
  • Improving our services based on anonymous feedback.

3. Confidentiality & Security

We maintain strict confidentiality standards. Your personal data is never sold, traded, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers to protect your information.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. Simply contact us at hello@saantham.com to make a request.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed.
